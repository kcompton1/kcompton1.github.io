About Us!

ABC Daycare is a fantastic place for your little one(s) to learn and grow. We know the first five years of life is very important for each child. That’s why we offer academic programs which are educational and fun. Throughout the day, children will learn about colors, alphabets, numbers, shapes, etc. These fundamental activities will shape a child’s future for the rest of their lives.

The food our children eat should be healthy and nutritious. ABC Daycare offers yummy meals and snacks daily for your children. We serve breakfast, am snacks, lunch, and pm snacks throughout the day. We provide healthy menus with whole grain, fruits, veggies, and 100% juices with less sugar. The menu consists of the food group guidelines provided by the State of California.

Exercise is very important in a child’s physical health. ABC Daycare offers fitness exercise throughout the day. A brief exercise helps children stay physically active. The exercise consists of jumping jacks, running in place, wall push-ups, hopping and stretching arms and legs. Children also have a chance to have free time so they are able to socialize and meet new friends.
