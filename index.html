Welcome to ABC Daycare!!!

Thank you for choosing ABC Daycare! We offer daycare and after school programs for your little one(s) aging from infants all the way to pre-k. Our mission is to help grow lifelong learners and confident individuals for the future. We feel each child is unique, so we are able to create a family-like atmosphere where each individual child can grow emotionally, academically, and physically, through this important part of their lives. ABC Daycare staff have experiences with degrees in Early Childhood Education. We encourage staff members to further their education and bring new and fresh ideas throughout the year.
